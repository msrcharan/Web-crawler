A man and woman have died after a fire at a house in Shropshire.Emergency crews were called to Glentworth Close, in Oswestry, at about 04:30 GMT, West Midlands Ambulance Service said.The woman, who was in a critical condition after being rescued from the fire, was treated at the scene before being taken to hospital where she died a short time later.The man was confirmed dead at the scene, paramedics added.Follow BBC West Midlands on Facebook, Twitter and Instagram.
